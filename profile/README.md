# 🚀 AstraInteractive

**AstraInteractive** is a collaborative space focused on building modern software with **Kotlin**.
Our projects range from **Minecraft plugins** to **Kotlin Multiplatform (KMM) applications**, including **Compose for Web** and backend services powered by **Ktor**.

> ⚠️ **Note:** This is not a formal organization — just a hub for open-source projects, primarily focused on Spigot development.

---

## 🛠️ What We Build

* 🎮 Minecraft Plugins (Spigot/Paper)
* 📱 Kotlin Multiplatform Apps (KMP)
* 🌐 Compose for Web projects
* ⚙️ Backend services using Ktor

---

## 📅 Contribution & Activity

We welcome contributions, ideas, and discussions!

> ⚠️ **Important:**
> Most **Pull Requests** and **Issues** are reviewed and handled during the **weekend (Saturday & Sunday)**.
